Trends in HCI Research 2025

11/21/2025

The Evolution of Accessibility

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) continues to evolve, with a strong focus on accessibility and inclusivity. Recent conferences have highlighted the importance of designing for diverse user needs, including those with visual impairments.

Key Areas of Interest

  1. Multimodal Interfaces: Combining haptic, auditory, and visual feedback to create richer experiences.
  2. AI-Assisted Accessibility: Using generative AI to provide real-time image descriptions and navigation assistance.
  3. Digital Nature: Exploring the boundary between digital and physical realities, a concept championed by researchers like Yoichi Ochiai.

As a researcher in this field, I am particularly interested in how we can use technology to bridge the gap for users with disabilities.
